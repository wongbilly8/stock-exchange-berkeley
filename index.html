    Description
    International Business Machines Corporation consists of five business segments: Global Technology Services (GTS), Global Business Services (GBS), Software, Systems Hardware, and Global Financing. Once seen as a strong competitor in the systems hardware manufacturing department, the company now focuses mainly on its GTS and GBS segments, providing Software & Information Technology Consulting to large and small business enterprises. In fact, Global Services (GTS/GBS) account for 60% of revenue, Software accounts for 28% of revenue, Systems Hardware accounts for 9% of revenue, and Global Financing accounts for 2% of revenue.
