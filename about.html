About  us

We are Stock Exchange at Berkeley,
a student-run organization for ticker dicsussions.




Discussions

Our goal is to engage members in ticker discussions and produce informed investment decisions from knowledgeable investors.




How It Works

Each week, our homepage is updated with a hand-crafted fundamental analysis and/or technical analysis on a publicly-traded company. Members will discuss on this particular company in our weekly meetings, debating whether or not it's stock is a good investment.




Heterogeneity

We seek members from a multiplicity of majors to enhance the quality of our discussions by providing diversified perspectives. Thus, we are confident that you will be a valuable asset to our community.

Contact Us
